# Understand customers before building products

## Great startups are built around customer needs, not founder assumptions. Claude can help founders identify customer pain points, define ideal users, prioritize features, and design MVPs that maximize learning while minimizing wasted effort.
    
    1 Customer Discovery: Identify who experiences the problem most intensely.
    2 MVP Strategy: Build only what is necessary to validate demand.
    3 Feature Prioritization: Separate essential features from distractions.
    4 Execution Planning: Create a focused action plan for the next 30 days.
