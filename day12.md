# Day 12
## Build Your Complete Job Search & Personal Branding Toolkit
## Go beyond resumes and build a complete career growth system

### Modern hiring goes far beyond resumes. Recruiters evaluate communication, personal branding, outreach quality, positioning, and overall professional presence. Claude can help generate a complete toolkit that improves visibility, networking effectiveness, and interview opportunities.
    
    1 ATS Optimization: Create recruiter-friendly and ATS-compatible career assets.
    2 Recruiter Outreach: Generate professional outreach messages that increase response rates.
    3 Personal Branding: Build a strong professional identity and positioning statement.
    4 Career Strategy: Identify strengths, gaps, and growth opportunities.


# Prompts:
  You are an expert Technical Recruiter, Hiring Manager, Career Coach, Executive Resume Writer, and Personal Branding Consultant.
  Carefully analyze my resume, LinkedIn profile, portfolio, and professional background.
  Your objective is to create a complete job search and personal branding toolkit tailored to my profile.
  Requirements:
  * Use only information available in my resume/profile.
  * Identify and highlight my most relevant skills, achievements, certifications, projects, leadership experience, and measurable business impact.
  * Emphasize strengths that are most valuable for recruiters and hiring managers in my target industry.
  * Adapt the messaging to my experience level (student, fresher, experienced professional, manager, or executive).
  * Use a professional, modern, recruiter-focused, and results-oriented writing style.
  * Avoid generic AI-generated language.
  * Quantify achievements whenever possible.
  * Ensure all content is ATS-friendly and ready to use immediately.
  
  Generate the following:
  
  SECTION 1:
  ATS-Friendly Cover Letter
  (350–450 words)
  
  SECTION 2:
  Recruiter Outreach Email
  
  SECTION 3:
  Hiring Manager Email
  
  SECTION 4:
  LinkedIn Connection Request
  (Maximum 300 characters)
  
  SECTION 5:
  Referral Request Message
  
  SECTION 6:
  Follow-Up Email
  (Send after 5 days if no response)
  
  SECTION 7:
  30-Second Professional Introduction
  (Self-introduction for interviews, networking, and career events)
  
  SECTION 8:
  Top 10 Job Titles Best Suited for My Profile
  
  SECTION 9:
  Key Strengths Recruiters Will Notice
  
  SECTION 10:
  Skill Gap Analysis
  
  * Current strengths
  * Missing skills
  * Recommended learning roadmap
  
  SECTION 11:
  Personal Brand Summary
  
  * Unique value proposition
  * Professional positioning statement
  * Recruiter headline
  
  SECTION 12:
  Interview Talking Points
  
  * Most impressive achievements
  * Key stories to discuss
  * Areas recruiters are likely to ask about
  
  Make every section highly personalized based on my profile and generate content that is ready to send or use immediately without requiring edits.


# Output:
Section 1 — Resume Audit
5 specific strengths identified in your current resume
6 actionable fixes (live URLs, ATS anchors, AWS specifics, etc.)
An optimized professional summary, ready to paste
ATS keyword bank across 3 categories (Data Engineering, ML, Cloud)

Section 2 — LinkedIn Makeover
2 headline options (role-first vs value-first)
Full About section draft with the "→" formatting that works on LinkedIn
Featured section pinning strategy
15+ skills to add, recommendations playbook

Section 3 — Cover Letters
3 templates: Data Engineer · ML Engineer · Data Analyst
Each framed around your specific projects and metrics — not generic

Section 4 — Interview Prep
5 master STAR stories mapped to your real experience
5 technical questions with model answers (XGBoost vs RF, RAG vs fine-tuning, data leakage, SQL windows)
Behavioral question handling
4 smart questions to ask interviewers

Section 5 — Personal Brand Strategy
Your differentiator positioning ("full-stack ownership")
GitHub profile action checklist
4-week LinkedIn content calendar
30-second elevator pitch script

Section 6 — Job Search Strategy
3-tier role targeting (best fit → stretch)
Target company categories for India market (MNC, Healthcare AI, startups)
Salary expectations with negotiation guidance
30-60-90 day execution plan
