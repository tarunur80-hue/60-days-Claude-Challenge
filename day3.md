# Role based prompts:

Role prompting is a powerful technique that guides LLM's behavior by assigning it specific roles, enhancing the style, accuracy, and depth of its outputs. 
Whether you're aiming for a certain tone, improving clarity, or eliciting complex and nuanced responses, role prompting can be of help. By adhering to best
practices and being aware of potential pitfalls, you can fully leverage role prompting, making your interactions with language models more effective and 
tailored to your specific needs.
